Cambium · the first session

The 20-interaction onboarding tutorial

The operator is an infinite game; the founder is the player; every good game opens with a tutorial that teaches the loop while it hooks. These first 20 interactions are engineered against the Octalysis 8 core drives — leading White Hat (intrinsic, sustainable) and using Black Hat only where grounded in something real (the solvency bound, the live market, actual drift). Each step also teaches one piece of the micro/meso/macro loop and reveals one operator capability.

01The Octalysis octagon — mapped to the operator

White Hat (top) = sustainable, intrinsic. Black Hat (bottom) = urgent, can burn out. Left Brain = extrinsic/logic; Right Brain = intrinsic/emotion.

White Hat (sustainable) Black Hat (grounded only)

Mid-brain → noesis. The vertical axis — drives 1 (Epic Meaning) ↕ 8 (Loss & Avoidance), i.e. meaning ↔ survival — is the mid-brain. Its activation bypasses the routine tick and invokes noesis, the operator's meaning/intuition layer: it reflects the why, speaks in the founder's deepest register, and escalates to the human when the stakes are real. Tutorial noesis peaks: #1 · #13 · #16 · #17 · #18 · #20.

Guardrails — so the tutorial isn't a casino:

  • No fake scarcity. Every "limited" is the real solvency runway / a real viability bound.
  • No manufactured loss. #18 fires only on a real V-spike, never on a script.
  • No engagement for its own sake. Every step teaches a real capability and advances the venture.
  • The last feeling is Calling, not anxiety. #20 is deliberately White Hat — you leave owning an infinite game, not fearing a timer.
