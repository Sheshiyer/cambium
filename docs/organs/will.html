👔 will — operate + go to market

The executive will of the body: a 7-agent C-suite that runs the business multi-tenant, governs spend, and turns a finished artifact into a go-to-market motion. One organ of the organism — not the whole OS, just its operating will.

Sheshiyer/snow-gloves-os will / business-ops artifact → business 💲 gated (Explee)

1Overview

The operating layer: who decides, who routes, who guards the money.

will is a 7-agent C-suite — ceo, chief-of-staff, cto, dispatcher, interpreter, librarian, sentinel — wrapped in multi-tenant governance and a go-to-market motion. The dispatcher routes incoming work through workflows/skill-hooks.yaml (the file routes 60 skills → 7 agents, orchestrated by Chief of Staff), so the CEO and CTO stay strategic while the right skill reaches the right agent for the right tenant.

Every action is tenant-scoped, audit-logged, and approval-gated where risk or spend is involved. The organ takes a finished artifact and the brand it belongs to, then operates it as a running business — onboarding tenants, governing the skill graph, and launching GTM via brand_to_gtm.

2Internal architecture — the self-similar Φ

One input, one conductor hub, many spokes, one output — the same fractal as every other organ.

4Data flow + contract

From a finished artifact to a list of ranked candidates and a running distribution motion.

artifact + brand-docs (+ approved Brief) │ ▼ dispatcher ──route──▶ gtm.py :: brand_to_gtm(brief) │ brand-docs ▸ synthesized filters ▼ Explee /search/companies + /search/people ⟵ approval-gated │ ▼ ranked candidates + distribution = a running business

The contract is artifact → business: brand_to_gtm reads the brand-docs, synthesizes Explee filters, and queries companies + people through the explee-proxy — only behind an approval. Output is a ranked candidate set wired into the distribution OS.

5Homeostasis role

The other organs keep a single artifact on-brand. will is a homeostat one level up: it keeps the whole business on-brand at the portfolio scale, across every tenant. It is the OS — the executive that operates the body multi-tenant — yet it is still ONE organ, not the whole organism.

Its corrective loop is governance: brand_to_gtm is approval-gated per constitution #4 — no unapproved spend off the path. The organ cannot reach paid Explee, or launch a GTM motion, without an approved Brief flowing through approvals.py. That gate is how the will stays bounded while it scales the business.
